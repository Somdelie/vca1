About Us

Vleikop Chartered and Accountant Inc. INC (“VCA”) is an indigenous black firm founded in the Republic of South Africa. Our business activities are focused on auditing, accounting, advisory and taxation services and are not restricted in nature. MAA is a firm of Chartered Accountants that provides technical advisory services to audit firms and companies. These services include tax opinions, IFRS opinions as well as in-house seminars. We are a young company looking to push the boundaries of excellence and committed to delivering exceptional service to its clients. Due to the size of our company, every client gets special attention and is treated as if it is the only client. We are also a certified level 1 BBBEE contributor and 100% Black owned and managed. MAA provides innovative solutions to both public and private sector clients including those listed on the Johannesburg Stock Exchange.
